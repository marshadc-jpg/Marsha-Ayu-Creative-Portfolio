TrueColors is a zine series that highlights & follows the journey of youth in growing and finding their place in society and community. In the first volume ‘Blooms When We Are’, we focused the theme on self-development, emotions & growth. While also using flowers as symbols to a much deeper mechanism & analogy to our distinctively creative process. We want to let our participants freely express & implement their ideas in our zine series by giving them a platform to do so; a look into the daily life & progress of The TrueColors.
