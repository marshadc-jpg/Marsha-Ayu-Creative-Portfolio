ARTIST STATEMENT SKILLS WORKS

Marsha Ayu is a Visual Artist / Designer based in Jakarta, Indonesia. She’s starting her artistic journey by curating exhibitions & making her own magazine series. She has organized classes as well as workshops related to art & design.

Throughout the last 6 years of being involved in a more artistic background, she has explored several mediums to enhance her skills & knowledge in mentioned areas. She is keen about art management, graphic design & editorial fields. Her works range from: zine, poetry, video art, mix media installations and others.

The themes and issues in her earlier works mainly talking about self exploration. Her inspiration comes from social & personal issues that have a potential to be transformed into an art piece. She enjoys volunteering, curating & discussion forums. In the future, she sees herself working as an artist, perhaps a curator or a designer.
